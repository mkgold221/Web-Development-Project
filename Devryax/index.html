FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Website & App Development FAQs

Website Development
App Development

How long does it take to develop a website? +

The time it takes to create your website depends on its complexity and the features you want. A basic website with a few pages and standard functionality can typically be completed within 2 to 4 Days. If you need a more advanced site — such as an eCommerce platform, or one with custom development — it might take 6 to 12 Days to ensure everything is properly designed, developed, tested, and optimized.

What is the pricing for your web development services? +

Our pricing is project-based and depends on factors such as the site's complexity, number of pages, and required features. We offer affordable packages to ensure we work with your budget. We've also got discounts running all year long. Please check out our Pricing page or contact us for a customized quote!

What types of websites do you create? +

We build all types of websites, including Corporate, E-Commerce, Educational, Real Estate, Fintech, Blogs, Portfolio sites, and many more. Whatever your needs are, we create websites that suit your requirements and taste.

Do you provide post-launch maintenance? +

After your website goes live, we offer a month of technical support and website maintenance at no extra cost. This ensures your site remains secure, updated, and optimized for performance. After the first month, you can also choose a maintenance plan that suits your needs to keep our support going.

What do you need from me to get started? +

To get started, we'll need details about your business operations, content (text, images), and any design preferences you have. If you already have a domain and hosting, great! If not, we can assist you purchase them. Once we have these, we'll begin the development process right away!

How long does it take to develop a mobile app? +

Mobile app development timelines vary based on complexity. A simple app with basic features typically takes 5-6 Days, while more complex applications with advanced functionality, integrations, and custom UI/UX can take 8-10 weeks or more. We'll provide a detailed timeline after understanding your specific requirements.

Do you develop for both iOS and Android? +

Yes! We develop native apps for both iOS and Android platforms. We also offer cross-platform development using React Native and Flutter, which allows you to reach both platforms with a single codebase, reducing development time and cost while maintaining native-like performance.

What is included in your app development package? +

Our comprehensive app development package includes: UI/UX design, frontend and backend development, API integrations, testing across multiple devices, deployment to app stores, and post-launch support. We also provide documentation and training for your team.

How much does app development cost? +

App development costs range from $1,000 for simple apps to $50,000+ for complex enterprise applications. The final cost depends on features, platforms, design complexity, and integrations. We offer transparent pricing and can work with various budget ranges.

Do you help with app store submission? +

Absolutely! We handle the entire app store submission process for both Apple App Store and Google Play Store. This includes preparing all required assets, creating store listings, managing the submission process, and addressing any review feedback from the stores.

What ongoing support do you provide after app launch? +

We provide 3 months of free technical support after launch, including bug fixes and minor updates. After that, we offer flexible maintenance plans that include regular updates, security patches, performance monitoring, and compatibility updates for new OS versions.
