Our Story

A Culinary Journey Since 2005

Founded by award-winning chef Michael Laurent, Savoria has been serving exquisite cuisine for over 18 years. Our philosophy is simple: use the finest seasonal ingredients, prepare them with care, and serve them with passion.

Every dish tells a story of tradition, innovation, and the rich culinary heritage that inspires our team of talented chefs. We source our ingredients locally whenever possible, supporting sustainable farming practices.
